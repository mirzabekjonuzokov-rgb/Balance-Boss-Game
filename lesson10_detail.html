Lesson 10: Mock Writing Exam + Portfolio Review ??

?? Lesson Overview & Core Structure: THE FINAL TEST

Topic Focus: Science & Future. A challenging, integrative topic testing all skills.

Objective: To execute a Band 9-level essay under strict 40-minute conditions, demonstrating mastery of all structures and vocabulary learned throughout the course.

Final Exam Protocol

  1. Plan (5 mins): Idea generation and structure outline.
  2. Write (30 mins): Execute the full 250+ word essay.
  3. Review (5 mins): Proofread for grammar and lexical errors.

?? Grammar Focus: Review of all Advanced Structures

The goal is to naturally integrate a variety of complex sentence types, including conditionals, participles, noun clauses, relative clauses, and nominalization, while maintaining near-perfect accuracy.

  • Target: Use at least 3 different advanced structures naturally.
  • Accuracy: Aim for 95%+ grammatical accuracy.

Example of Integrated Structures (Band 9.0)

What is crucial for humanity’s progress is not only the development of green technology, which promotes innovation, but also the strict ethical considerations that must be adopted by all research institutions.

?? Key Vocabulary & Reward

Focus on **Precision** and **Academic Tone**. Use your highest-level collocations (e.g., conduct scientific research, address ethical concerns).

  • Portfolio Review: Compare your Lesson 1 essay with your Lesson 10 essay.
  • Award Ceremony: Presentation of the **500,000 UZS Top Writer Prize**!
